A Journey of Self-Discovery

Sacred Engagement is a self-discovery program for individuals sensing that life is asking more of them. It combines contemplative practice with community support — turning inward with awareness, extending outward in deep connection.

A Four-Module Journey
Module 1 — Waking Up Developing self-awareness and reflection practices
Module 2 — Clearing Away Removing obstacles between yourself and others
Module 3 — Breaking Through Experiencing life freed from judgment and fear
Module 4 — Stepping Into Integration and ongoing joy practice
